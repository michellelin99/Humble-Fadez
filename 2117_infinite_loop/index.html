ABOUT ME

Kelnitra Robinson

Kelnitra Robinson, affectionately known as “Kelz”, is a licensed Master Barber located in Nashville, Tennessee. Kelz has been cutting hair and servicing clients in the Nashville area for more than 6 years. Her love for barbering began many years ago when she started cutting her nephews’ hair. This love quickly evolved into a strong passion, which ultimately led Kelz to the classroom for formal barber education and training. Since that time, Kelz has worked alongside and been mentored by some of Nashville’s top barbers and barber influencers in the Atlanta, Houston, and Dallas barber industry. Kelz attends numerous barber conferences, classes, and hair shows throughout the year, as she is committed to perfecting her craft, expanding her knowledge, and fueling her creativity and passion. In fact, her creativity and passion is exactly what earned her a trophy for placing among the top 3 barbers in the Xotics Hair Battle in 2019. As a talented, gifted, award-winning barber, Kelz remains humble, and that humility is what inspired and birthed her brand Humble Fadez. Kelz is dedicated to building strong connections with her clients and helping them express their personalities through every cut. When Kelz is not creating waves in the barbershop, she is traveling, cooking, expanding her other businesses and spending time with her family. “Barbering saved my life”- Kelz Tha Barber
